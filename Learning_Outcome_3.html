At the start of designing the background for my portfolio, I began by looking for inspiration. I realized that this portfolio should connect with who I am and reflect my personality, so I thought about combining my interest in history and royal dynasties with a modern, clean design. I’ve always admired the "fleur-de-lis" symbol, a classic emblem used frequently in medieval heraldry. I came across a background on Adobe Stock featuring a fleur-de-lis pattern, which I tried using, but I noticed it didn’t quite fit my vision and had a watermark. So, I decided to design my own background on Canva, to make it feel more personal and fitting with my concept. I believe it’s essential in this industry to stand out and bring a unique touch as a specialist.
