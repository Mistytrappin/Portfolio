Blik project

In the BLIK project, I took on the role of a designer, focusing on creating prototypes and improving the overall look of the website. I also participated in presentations with stakeholders and prepared slides to showcase the design process, including fonts and prototypes I worked on. Throughout the project, I made sure to gather and apply feedback, testing different color schemes and layouts to create a user-friendly and visually appealing design. My goal was to ensure the website looked professional and met the needs of the project.
