Introduction

In this semester, I focused on expanding my skills in both web design and digital content creation, while also developing my social media presence. Through various projects, including creating prototypes, designing album covers, and producing visualizers for my music, I learned how to apply design principles in a more practical and personal context. Additionally, I gained experience in organizing and presenting my work, using tools like Figma, CapCut, Canva, and AI to create content. Throughout this process, I also managed to grow my social media following, significantly increasing my TikTok views and YouTube subscribers. This semester has been instrumental in enhancing both my designing and creative skills, allowing me to effectively combine design and digital marketing strategies.

Challenges and Improvements

One major challenge was optimizing images on my page. Large, unscaled images caused layout issues and slowed down loading times. However, I quickly realized that organizing my projects as PDFs would allow for a more structured and accessible way to present my designs. This change required adapting my workflow to ensure that the information was presented clearly and professionally. Another challenge was the iterative process of creating prototypes, as it required careful attention to detail and constant refinement to ensure that the designs met both usability and visual appeal standards..
