Hello, my name is Maks Mistewicz, but they call me Mi$ty.


I've been diving deep into my creative process, crafting new music that's pushing the boundaries of sound. My latest project, 'Panorama Snów' (which translates to 'Panorama of Dreams' in Polish), is an exploration of where house music meets the raw energy of rap. Imagine a sonic journey through a late-night cityscape, bathed in neon hues. That's the vibe I'm going for. 'Panorama Snów' features a blend of moody, atmospheric house melodies, punctuated by rhythmic, introspective rap verses. Deep, pulsating basslines provide the foundation, while the flow of the rap adds a layer of narrative and emotional depth.
